I'm an Interactive Developer. I live in the woods next to a river. I have a wife and a daughter; they are the inspiration for everything I do. I love generative art; I love dynamic visualization; I love emergent behavior. I believe there is pattern and form within all things and that it is beautiful.

About

Download my CV

My father was an artist and I spent my childhood watching him create in his studio. Since that point I myself have had an unending drive to create. I feel as though I'm suffocating if I am not generating something new and original. I've spent time working as a screen printer, a book binder, an iron worker, a freelance journalist, a textile designer, a web designer and a web/mobile/game developer.
